About

Founded by Kristoff Estefano, in Rotterdam The Netherlands in 2007, with a young and talented team, back then we visioned only a 10% of everything we have been able to do, in fact, this adventure is more than anything we could have ever dreamed of. Being surrounded by an incredible team of experts, with unique backgrounds such a sports education, professional gymnasts, graphic designers, translators, web developers, circus teachers and show producers, are the true key of this company having had such an incredible run. The day of today we are based in Netherlands, Germany, Switzerland, Spain, Ukraine, Mexico and Argentina.
