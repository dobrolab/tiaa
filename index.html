Our history

The Ramos Rodogell Family since 1840 is a remarkable lineage of circus artists, known for their extraordinary acrobatic and aerial performances. Let’s delve into their captivating story:

The Rodogels (Teeterboard Acrobats):

The Rodogels specialized in the teeterboard, a thrilling act that involves catapulting performers into the air using a seesaw-like apparatus. Their precision, timing, and daring flips left audiences in awe.

In 1980, they showcased their teeterboard skills at Billy Smart’s Christmas Circus1. Their gravity-defying feats exemplified the essence of circus artistry.

The Flying Ramos (Flying Trapeze Artists):

The Flying Ramos were masters of the flying trapeze, an act that combines athleticism, grace, and sheer courage. Suspended high above the audience, they executed breathtaking swings, somersaults, and mid-air catches.

Like the Rodogels, the Flying Ramos also graced the stage at Billy Smart’s Christmas Circus in 1981. Their performance epitomized the magic of the big top.

The legacy of the Ramos Rodogel Family lives on through their daring acts, leaving an indelible mark on the circus world. 🎪✨
